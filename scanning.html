Scanning

Why Choose Our Scanning Service?

We specialize in converting your physical documents, photos, and records into high-quality digital files. Our state-of-the-art scanning equipment ensures accurate, fast, and secure digitization, whether you’re preserving memories or streamlining your business operations.

How It Works

  1. Drop Off, Mail, or We Will Pick Up Your Files: Bring your files to us or we will come to you! Or, send them securely via mail.
  2. Scanning Process: We use high-resolution scanners to digitize your materials with precision.
  3. Delivery: Choose how you’d like your files delivered—via secure cloud storage or on a flash drive.
  4. Peace of Mind: Once scanned, you can opt for us to store your physical files, or we can securely shred them for you.

What Sets Us Apart?

  • High-Resolution Scanning: Crisp, clear digital copies of your documents and photos.
  • OCR Technology: Convert scanned documents into searchable and editable formats.
  • Fast Turnaround: Standard and expedited options to suit your needs.
  • Secure Handling: All files are processed in a secure facility to protect your privacy.
