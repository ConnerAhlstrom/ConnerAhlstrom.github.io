About us

Our Story

Amarillo is the central hub of life for the panhandle. With the rise of technology constantly expanding and transforming itself, it is difficult for smaller businesses to be able to deal with the upkeep of their information and documents. Digigalz LLC is a small business intended to help blue-collar businesses and workers transition from their physical files and upload them to a secure drive accessible to them at any request.

Digigalz aims to bridge the gap between the past and present by digitizing and maintaining documents. We work together to preserve history, culture, education, and communities. Our service will satisfy the need for updating business files whenever there is not time or enough hands to do it. We step in to make sure businesses can keep up with the rapidly growing technology advances and not fall behind by being trapped in papers.

How We Help You

Digigalz's proposed service is to assist blue-collared businesses by digitizing their old files. Services also include photo restoration, file scanning and sorting in digital and analog space, turning text-based files into a screen-reader-friendly format, document shredding, and package deals for file volume, upkeep of digital files in cloud storage.

  1. Digigalz will retrieve and or will be receiving the clients' physical files (mailed, dropped off, or received physically) which will be scanned and then transported onto a designated cloud system, powered by Microsoft Azure.
  2. Your files will be organized per your request
  3. Files are stored in the cloud and our security personnel will look over to ensure that the client's private information stays private.
  4. Digigalz will also offer the ability to keep these physical files if desired but also have the service of shredding your information. Beyond the files, we can also upload photos and restore them to our best abilities for later use.

Our solution will ensure that the client can keep up with the demands and competition from the outside world as it will be our responsibility to be in charge of their files. It will also be highly efficient for the usage of time, as the client can put in more time into other things without the worry of their files. 

Security

At Digigalz, we understand the importance of safeguarding your sensitive information. That’s why we utilize Microsoft's Azure’s enterprise-grade cloud platform, trusted by global organizations for its security features. Your data’s security isn’t just a priority—it’s our commitment.

How we make it happen

  1. Powered by Microsoft Azure
    • End-to-End Encryption: All your files are encrypted during storage and transit using Azure’s encryption standards.
    • Global Compliance: Microsoft Azure adheres to leading international compliance standards, including GDPR, HIPAA, and ISO 27001, ensuring your data is managed responsibly.
  2. Locking your data with precision
    • HSM (Hardware Security Modules): We use Azure Key Vault to generate and manage cryptographic keys securely, ensuring only authorized access to your files.
    • Automated Key Management: Regular key rotations and audits prevent vulnerabilities, keeping your data secure at every step.

Your Security, Guaranteed

At Digigalz, we don’t just meet industry standards—we exceed them. We offer you a service that’s secure, compliant, and future-proof.
