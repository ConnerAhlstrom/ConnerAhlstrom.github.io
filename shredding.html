Shredding Services

Why Choose Our Shredding Service?

When it’s time to dispose of old or unnecessary documents, ensure they’re shredded securely. We provide reliable shredding services to protect your sensitive information from falling into the wrong hands. From personal records to business paperwork, we offer flexible options to meet your needs.

What Can You Shred?

All types of paper, including office paper, receipts, notes, printouts, and more. And you don’t need to worry about removing staples, paper clips or folders. We can take the documents just as they are.

What can't I Shred?

  1. Cardboard
  2. CDs & DVDs
  3. Hardcover Books

Recycling documents

We share interest in protecting and preserving the planet. All destroyed materials are recycled so you can feel good about protecting your documents and the environment.
